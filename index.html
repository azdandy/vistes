Welcome to Vispalma Indonesia Perkasa

We are Vispalma Indonesia Perkasa, a company that supplies the various needs of our prospective buyers. As a company that is an aggregator in Indonesia, we work closely with farmers and manufacturers from various regions in Indonesia to provide various needs for example, spices, coffee, honey, and coconut and their derivatives.

Although e concentrate on sending various spice products to the international market but we do not rule out the possibility for us to supply to the national market according to client requests. Our company also always makes improvements in the implementation of production and distribution processes in order to achieve the best level of effectiveness and efficiency with the aim of course providing the best quality for our clients in order to create long term contracts full of trust.
